Our Tutor Specialities

We specialise in students with special needs and can provide tutoring for the core subjects: English and Maths from Year 3 to Year 11 (including GCSEs). Also 11+ exam preparation.

We can also cover other subjects on request, including life skills and functional skills for Year 12 right through to adult education.

We are available days and evenings, weekends and during the school holidays.

Available for home tuition or the more flexible online tuition.
